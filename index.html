Privacy Policy for URL Replacer

Effective Date: 9 October 2024

Introduction

Welcome to URL Replacer! This Privacy Policy outlines how our Chrome extension interacts with your data to provide its functionality. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

Information We Process

URL Replacer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser to replace specified URLs when you paste text into input fields and contentEditable elements on various websites.

How It Works

  • Local Processing: All URL replacement operations are performed locally on your device. The extension reads the text you paste and replaces specified URLs based on your configurations.
  • No Data Transmission: No data is sent to external servers or third parties. All processing is done in real-time within your browser.
  • User-Defined Settings: The URLs you specify for replacement are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. These settings are not shared or transmitted.

Permissions

URL Replacer requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab to inject scripts for URL replacement.
  • scripting: Enables the extension to execute scripts within the context of web pages.
  • storage: Used to save and retrieve your URL replacement configurations.

Security

We prioritize your security and privacy. Since URL Replacer does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is minimal risk associated with its use. All operations are confined to your local browser environment.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
